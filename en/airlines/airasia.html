About AirAsia Group

AirAsia Group, founded 2001, is SE Asia's largest LCC. Operating exclusively on A320 family across Malaysia, Thailand, Indonesia, Philippines, and India. Regular foreign pilot hiring. Group entities include AirAsia and AirAsia X (long-haul).
