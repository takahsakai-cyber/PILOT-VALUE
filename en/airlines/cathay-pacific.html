About Cathay Pacific

Cathay Pacific, founded 1946, is the Hong Kong flag carrier. Hubbing at Hong Kong International Airport (HKG), it serves 60+ countries and 100+ cities as a Oneworld core member.

Promotions are on pure seniority — typically 12+ years to captain. While slower, the system is transparent and predictable. Direct Entry F/O hiring is active and pick-up pace has recovered in 2025–2026.

Routes & Fleet

Routes

HKG hub to 100+ cities. Japan (NRT/HND/OSA/NGO/FUK) frequent direct flights. Europe, North America, Australia, Middle East, Southeast Asia, South Asia. All international.

Fleet

A350-900/1000, B777-300ER, B777-200, A321neo

Training

Training & Checkrides

Type Rating

Cathay Pacific City training center adjacent to HKG. Latest generation simulators.

Pure Seniority System

All upgrades, aircraft bids, and schedule bids decided purely by seniority number. Transparent.

Second Officer (SO) Program

Low-hours entry via SO position. Experience accumulates before F/O promotion.

Captain Upgrade (Command)

Typically 12–16+ years. Pure seniority-based — predictable given company size and demand.

Recurrent Checks

Proficiency checks 1–2x per year. Civil Aviation Department (CAD) standards.

Life in Hong Kong

English is one of two official languages. Japanese food and community available. High living costs are a challenge.
